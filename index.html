My Journey

Curious about products, serious about execution.

A single story of how I think, build, research, and grow as a product-focused technology student.

I am a B.E. Information Technology student at the University of Mumbai, passionate about building meaningful digital products from scratch. I am the Founder of Tirth Sutra, a platform focused on creating authentic and spiritually rooted experiences.

I have also gained startup experience as a Product Manager and Tech Assistant at RentZoo, a startup founded by IIT Bombay students that is building digital rental systems for heavy machinery booking. This experience helped me understand product thinking, user problems, startup execution, and the importance of building practical solutions.

My main interest lies in product development, startup research, system design, and user experience. I enjoy exploring new startup ideas, researching existing markets, and building products that solve real problems at scale. I am especially interested in creating authentic, original, and impactful solutions that can contribute meaningfully to the country.

From a technical perspective, I am interested in designing large-scale systems that can handle a high number of users efficiently. I also care deeply about creating clean, simple, and user-friendly interfaces that provide the best possible user experience.

My long-term goal is to work on impactful products, contribute to strong startup ecosystems, and grow into roles focused on Product Building, System Design, and Startup Innovation.

I build with both code and business clarity.

I can understand technical execution, but I also ask why the product should exist, who it serves, how it can grow, and what makes it useful in the real world.

This helps me build with a founder mindset. Whether I am working on Tirth Sutra, RentZoo, or a new idea, I try to connect product thinking, system design, user experience, and business practicality. My goal is not just to create something that works, but something that is meaningful, scalable, and valuable.
